# Business Foundations Audit

Use this skill to evaluate core business fundamentals and identify missing building blocks for sustainable growth.

## Trigger Conditions
- User unsure why business growth is stalled
- User asks what fundamentals are missing
- User wants structured business health assessment

## Required Inputs
1. Business model and current stage
2. Product/service and target customer
3. Revenue/cost basics (high-level is fine)
4. Current operating model and team setup

## Workflow
1. **Foundation scan**: evaluate positioning, offer clarity, channel fit, operations, financial discipline.
2. **Gap diagnosis**: rank weakest links and root causes.
3. **Priority roadmap**: sequence fixes by impact and effort.
4. **Execution plan**: define owners, timelines, and success criteria.
5. **Review cadence**: establish monthly scorecard checks.

## Output Format
- Foundation scorecard
- Top critical gaps
- 90-day remediation roadmap
- KPI checklist for monitoring

## Guardrails
- Don’t overcomplicate early-stage businesses.
- Separate symptoms from root causes.
- Focus first on constraints that block growth most.
