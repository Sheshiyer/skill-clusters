# Product Launch Strategy

Use this skill to prepare a structured launch plan that maximizes awareness, adoption, and learning.

## Trigger Conditions
- User launching a new product or feature
- User asks for launch planning
- User needs pre-launch/post-launch strategy

## Required Inputs
1. Product summary and target audience
2. Launch date and constraints
3. Success criteria (signups, sales, activation)
4. Team/resources and channels available

## Workflow
1. **Launch objective**: define what success means for this launch.
2. **Audience + positioning**: clarify segment and message hierarchy.
3. **Phased rollout**: pre-launch, launch week, post-launch optimization.
4. **Channel orchestration**: assign tactics by channel and timeline.
5. **Feedback + iteration**: set up rapid signal collection and response.

## Output Format
- Launch strategy one-pager
- Week-by-week launch timeline
- Channel tactic checklist
- Risks and contingency plan
- Post-launch metric review framework

## Guardrails
- Prioritize focus over channel sprawl.
- Make timeline realistic to team bandwidth.
- Include explicit contingency scenarios.
