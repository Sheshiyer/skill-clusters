# Specialization vs Diversification Advisor

Use this skill to decide whether to focus deeper or expand offerings.

## Trigger Conditions
- User asks whether to diversify or specialize
- User evaluating product line expansion
- User needs strategic focus decision

## Required Inputs
1. Current offerings and performance
2. Market opportunities
3. Team capacity and capabilities
4. Risk appetite and growth goals

## Workflow
1. Evaluate current traction and defensibility.
2. Assess expansion opportunities and complexity.
3. Compare strategic scenarios.
4. Recommend path with milestones.
5. Define decision review triggers.

## Output Format
- Scenario comparison
- Recommended strategy
- Execution milestones
- Risk controls

## Guardrails
- Avoid expansion without operational readiness.
- Prevent overfocus if core market is shrinking.
- Tie choice to evidence and constraints.
