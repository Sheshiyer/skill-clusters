# Viral Campaign Ideator

Use this skill to brainstorm campaigns designed for attention, sharing, and audience participation.

## Trigger Conditions
- User asks for viral campaign ideas
- User wants high-reach creative concepts
- User seeks social-first growth ideas

## Required Inputs
1. Product/audience context
2. Campaign goal
3. Brand voice/risk tolerance
4. Channel constraints

## Workflow
1. Define virality objective and metric.
2. Generate hook-led campaign concepts.
3. Add participation and sharing mechanics.
4. Evaluate fit, risk, and execution feasibility.
5. Recommend pilot and amplification plan.

## Output Format
- 5-10 campaign ideas
- Why each could spread
- Risk assessment and safeguards
- Test launch plan

## Guardrails
- Avoid gimmicks misaligned with brand.
- Balance novelty with clarity.
- Consider moderation and backlash risks.
