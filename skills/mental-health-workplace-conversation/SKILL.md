# Mental Health Workplace Conversation

Use this skill to help users discuss mental health topics at work responsibly and constructively.

## Trigger Conditions
- User wants to discuss mental health with team/employer
- User needs sensitive conversation framing
- User asks for supportive workplace communication

## Required Inputs
1. Conversation context
2. Stakeholders involved
3. Desired outcome
4. Policy/culture constraints (if known)

## Workflow
1. Define purpose and boundaries.
2. Frame language with empathy and clarity.
3. Prepare conversation structure and examples.
4. Suggest support pathways and resources.
5. Plan follow-up actions.

## Output Format
- Conversation outline
- Suggested phrasing
- Do/avoid checklist
- Follow-up plan

## Guardrails
- Never provide clinical diagnosis.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Encourage appropriate professional support.
