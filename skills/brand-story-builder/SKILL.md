# Brand Story Builder

Use this skill to create emotionally resonant, strategy-aligned brand storytelling.

## Trigger Conditions
- User asks for brand story
- User needs narrative-led marketing messaging
- User wants to humanize brand positioning

## Required Inputs
1. Brand purpose and audience
2. Customer pain/problem
3. Product/service role in transformation
4. Tone and channel context

## Workflow
1. Define protagonist and tension.
2. Frame conflict and stakes.
3. Position brand as guide/enabler.
4. Show transformation and proof.
5. Adapt story for channels.

## Output Format
- Core brand story narrative
- Short/medium/long variants
- Messaging pillars linked to story
- CTA suggestions

## Guardrails
- Keep story customer-centric.
- Avoid cliché or inflated claims.
- Use concrete transformation outcomes.
